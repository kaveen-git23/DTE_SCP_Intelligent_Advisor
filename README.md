# THE PROBLEM & SOLUTION
Objective: Reduce procurement spend by guiding buyers to lower-cost, pre-approved catalog items.
Current Issues: The Problem
- Buyers purchase from vendor websites at non‑negotiated prices. Missing cheaper catalog options.
- ~92% of sourcing bypass SRM catalogs, only 8% using catalogs.
- 54% of requisition are raised in Maximo, not SRM
- No visibility into catalog vs vendor pricing.
- Manual, discretionary sourcing with no price comparison.

Solution: (Future state)
- AI Advisor recommends cheapest approved options as the buyer requisition.
- Buyer prompts the advisor for the material they needed.
- AI search scans approved SRM catalogs for matches
- Recommend lower-cost, pre-approved equivalent with price
- Enables one‑click order placement.
Note: $1.1M Estimated savings from shifting spend to catalog guided sourcing – with $0.8M additional savings as catalog expand.

# SCOPE
In Scope:
- Integration with Maximo & SAP/SRM
- AI recommendations from catalog products
- Catalog expansion for approved vendors
- Intelligent advisor Performance dashboard & value tracking and key metrics with drill through capabilities.

Out of Scope:
- Purchase requisition auto‑creation
- Sole-catalog and sole-vendor sourcing strategy
- search through Non‑catalog vendor websites
- Inventory items & contract spend
